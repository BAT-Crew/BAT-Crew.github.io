Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2024

Introduction

At Vestin, operated by BAT, we highly value your privacy and are committed to ensuring the protection and responsible use of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect your personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Simulation Investment Data: Information related to your investment simulations within the app.
  • Profile and Nickname: Your Vestin profile details, including your chosen nickname.
  • Google Analytics (GA) Data: Data collected via Google Analytics to understand app usage and user behavior.
  • Purchase Information: Details of any in-app purchases made.
  • Reward Tab Activities: Information about your interactions with the Reward tab in the app.
  • Advertising ID (IDFA): With your consent, we collect IDFA for personalized advertising purposes.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: To enhance and personalize your experience on Vestin and to develop new features.
  • User Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Analytics and Research: To conduct analysis and research to improve our service.
  • Security Measures: To detect and prevent fraud and other illegal activities.
  • Personalized Advertising: To provide personalized advertisements through our advertising partners using IDFA.

Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These include:

  • Secure Storage: Your personal data is stored in secure networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights and are required to keep the information confidential.
  • Data Encryption: Sensitive data transmitted to and from our app is encrypted.

Information Sharing

We may share your IDFA with our advertising partners for the purpose of providing personalized advertisements. This sharing is done only with your explicit consent.

Changes to Privacy Policy
